About Us

Back in the early days of the pandemic in the Philippines in 2020, Clifton Guitars initiated its business venture by promoting musical instruments through online advertising and selling products on various online shopping apps. The owner of Clifton Guitars, CEO Adrian Tan, successfully navigated the challenges. As of October 2023, the company has achieved a significant milestone with the establishment of its physical store located at Aurora Boulevard, Cubao, Quezon City.
